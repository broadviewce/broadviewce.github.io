Writ of Election for County Sheriff, Seats 1 & 4 of the Broadview County Commission


November 20, 2023

   WHEREAS, on December 12th, County Sheriff SOURCED_V resigned from office and such resignation was submitted with immediate effect; and,

   WHEREAS, on December 12th, Commissioner Dominodownload resigned from office and such resignation was submitted with immediate effect; and,

   WHEREAS, on December 12th, Commissioner VampAtTaxEvasion resigned from office and such resignation was submitted with immediate effect; and,

   WHEREAS, §204.6 of the Code of Ordinances empowers the County Executive to "issue all writs of elections to the Florida Department of State's Elections Division."

   I, TaxesArentAwesome, by virtue of the authority vested in me by the Charter of Broadview County and other applicable laws in my official capacity as County Executive of Broadview County, Florida, in order to fill the vacancy caused by the resignations of SOURCED_V, Dominodownload, and VampAtTaxEvasion on December 12, 2023, do hereby issue this writ of election to direct the Florida Department of State's Elections Division to publish the call of election to all appropriate municipalities that are represented by the offices with vacancy as described with an effective date so as that such election occurs on Tuesday, December 19th, 2023.
